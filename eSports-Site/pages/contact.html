Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my team for tournament consideration?

Teams can submit applications through our tournament registration system. Visit the tournaments page and click on "Register" for any upcoming events. Make sure your team meets the eligibility requirements listed for each tournament.

Do you offer sponsorship opportunities?

Yes, we offer various sponsorship packages for tournaments, teams, and content creators. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@esportshub.com to discuss available opportunities and custom packages.

How can I become a content creator for eSports Hub?

We're always looking for talented content creators to join our team. Send us your portfolio and examples of your work to media@esportshub.com. We'll review your application and get back to you within 2 weeks.

What games do you cover?

We cover a wide range of competitive games including League of Legends, Dota 2, Counter-Strike 2, Valorant, Overwatch 2, Fortnite, and many more. Our coverage includes tournaments, news, team profiles, and player statistics.

How can I report a bug or technical issue?

Please report any technical issues to our support team at support@esportshub.com or use our live chat feature. Include details about the issue, your browser, and any error messages you've encountered.

Do you offer API access for developers?

Yes, we provide API access for developers who want to integrate eSports data into their applications. Contact our technical team at api@esportshub.com to learn more about our API offerings and pricing.
